Case Study

G Vote: Streamlined University Elections

G Vote simplifies and streamlines university elections by replacing manual, in-person voting processes with a secure, transparent online system.

The Problem

  • In-person voting can enable cheating and ballot tampering.
  • Long queues and conflicts reduce participation and trust.
  • Manual counting introduces delays and human error.

The Solution

  • Verified voter authentication and unique ballots.
  • Encrypted vote storage with tamper-proof records.
  • Real-time results with audit trails for transparency.

Impact

  • Eliminates opportunities for cheating and manipulation.
  • Reduces logistical overhead and prevents conflicts.
  • Accelerates result publication and improves confidence.
